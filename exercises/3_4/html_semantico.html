Fatos sobre o Stomatopoda

Informações gerais sobre o Stomatopoda (nome científico: Odontodactylus scyllarus)

Reino: Animalia
Filo: Arthropoda
Subfilo: Crustacea
Classe: Malacostraca
Subclasse: Hoplocarida
Ordem: Stomatopoda
Latreille, 1817

All mantis shrimp species can be divided into ‘spearers’ or ‘smashers’, based on the types of claws they have and tactics they use to kill the prey

Spearers have spiny appendages with barbed tips, that are used to stab soft-bodied prey, such as different types of worms and fish. Smashers have more developed club-like appendages that are used to blugeon and smash their prey to pieces.
